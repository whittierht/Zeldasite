About

Hyrule Compendium Explorer is your ultimate reference tool for exploring the vast worlds of Breath of the Wild and Tears of the Kingdom. Designed for dedicated Zelda fans and new players alike, this site lets you browse in-game data, search items, and track your favorite discoveries — all in one place.

Built using the Hyrule Compendium API, the site delivers quick access to detailed information on creatures, materials, equipment, and more. Whether you're striving for 100% completion or need help tracking elusive in-game content, Hyrule Compendium Explorer keeps essential data at your fingertips.

The site features a responsive design, accessible UI, and the ability to save favorites with local storage, making it a reliable companion whether you're on desktop or mobile. Our simple category navigation, A-Z index, and powerful filters ensure you can find exactly what you need, fast.

Join fellow adventurers in mastering Hyrule's secrets — browse, search, and explore outside the game whenever you need.
